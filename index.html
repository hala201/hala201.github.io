Research in Computational Social Sciences

Deep Learning for Behavioral Game Theory
Greg d'Eon, Hala Murad, Kevin Leyton-Brown, James Wright
Elementary GameNet is a neural network that predicts human non-strategic behavior in normal-form games (accepted into AAAI'26).
AI Sparring Partners: Alternate Search Strategies in Chess
Karim Hamade, Hala Murad, Ashton Anderson
Maia is a skill-compatible chess agent, we are developing a chess agent that acts as a sparring partner by tricking its human opponent (Work in prgress).

Research in Computater Graphics

StrokeStrip: Joint Parameterization and Fitting of Stroke Clusters
Dave Pagurek van Mossel, Chenxi Liu, Nicholas Vining, Mikhail Bessmeltsev, Alla Sheffer
StrokeStrip is a robust method for fitting intended curves to vector-format stroke clusters consistent with the viewer's expectation. I was briefly involved in building a web-interface for the Stroke Strip tool.
Fossa Finder: Virtual Reality for Anatomy Simulation
Metanaut Labs, UBC Emerging Media Lab
Fossa Finder is an interactive educational virtual reality tool to aid anatomy education with regards to the Pterygopalatine Fossa in collaboration with the UBC School of Dentistry and Metanaut Labs.
